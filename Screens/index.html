Capstone Class/Project · CSE140

PacMan: Capture the Flag

Developed intelligent agents for a competitive Capture the Flag variant of Pac-Man. The project required designing both offensive and defensive agents capable of strategic decision-making in a dynamic, adversarial environment. Our team implemented the A* search algorithm to optimize pathfinding and real-time gameplay decisions. The agents successfully competed against other teams, earning 5th place in the final tournament.

AI · Python · A* 2026 Group Project

Demo Walkthrough

In this demo, the agents navigate the maze in real-time. The offensive agent prioritizes collecting food and capturing the opponent's flag, while the defensive agent guards our territory and intercepts opponents. Observe how A* pathfinding allows intelligent movement around obstacles and dynamically reacts to opponent actions.

The agents’ decision-making highlights strategies like risk assessment, threat evaluation, and cooperative behavior. This setup demonstrates both the AI logic and the competitive dynamics in a live game environment.
